About SITS

Established in 2008, Sinhgad Institute of Technology and Science (SITS) in Narhe, Pune, is dedicated to nurturing both proficient engineers and responsible citizens. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE, SITS offers undergraduate programs in Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Telecommunication, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering.
